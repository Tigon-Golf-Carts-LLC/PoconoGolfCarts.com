EVOLUTION D5 MAVERICK 4 Overview

The EVOLUTION D5 MAVERICK 4 is a Evolution electric golf cart available now at Pocono Golf Carts in Pocono Pines, PA, with a manufacturer's suggested retail price (MSRP) starting at $8,995. It seats 4 passengers. The cart offers a top speed of 25 mph, about 30-45 miles of range per charge, a recharge time of roughly 6-8 hours. Payload capacity is rated at 1,000 lbs. It's a practical choice for a mix of street-legal commuting and off-road versatility.

Key Features

Powerful & Efficient Performance

Features front double wishbone independent suspension and rear leaf spring suspension, ensuring a smooth and stable ride. Rolls on 14x7 inch aluminum wheels with 23x10-14 inch silent off-road tires.
