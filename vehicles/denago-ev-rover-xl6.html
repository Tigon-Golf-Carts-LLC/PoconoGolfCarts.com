DENAGO EV ROVER XL6 Overview

The DENAGO EV ROVER XL6 is a Denago electric golf cart available now at Pocono Golf Carts in Pocono Pines, PA, with a manufacturer's suggested retail price (MSRP) starting at $12,995. It seats 6 passengers. The cart offers a top speed of 25 mph, about 40-55 miles of range per charge, a recharge time of roughly 6-8 hours. Payload capacity is rated at 800 lbs. It's a practical choice for off-road adventures and all-terrain exploration.

As an authorized Denago dealer serving the Pocono Mountains, our team can help you compare the DENAGO EV ROVER XL6 against similar models, explain street-legal requirements for low-speed vehicles in Pennsylvania, and arrange a test drive.
