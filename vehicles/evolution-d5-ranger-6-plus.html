EVOLUTION D5 RANGER 6 PLUS Overview

The EVOLUTION D5 RANGER 6 PLUS is a Evolution electric golf cart available now at Pocono Golf Carts in Pocono Pines, PA, with a manufacturer's suggested retail price (MSRP) starting at $11,595. It seats 2-6 passengers. The cart offers a top speed of 19 mph, about 30-45 miles of range per charge, a recharge time of roughly 6-8 hours. Payload capacity is rated at 800 lbs. It's a practical choice for a mix of street-legal commuting and off-road versatility.
