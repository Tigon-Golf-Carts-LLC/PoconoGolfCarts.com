EVOLUTION CLASSIC 4 PLUS Overview

The EVOLUTION CLASSIC 4 PLUS is a Evolution electric golf cart available now at Pocono Golf Carts in Pocono Pines, PA, with a manufacturer's suggested retail price (MSRP) starting at $6,795. The cart offers a top speed of 25 mph (LSV Package). It's a practical choice for neighborhood cruising and short street-legal trips.

As an authorized Evolution dealer serving the Pocono Mountains, our team can help you compare the EVOLUTION CLASSIC 4 PLUS against similar models, explain street-legal requirements for low-speed vehicles in Pennsylvania, and arrange a test drive. Every cart we sell is backed by local service and support—not just an online checkout. Call 1-844-844-6638 or request a quote to confirm current EVOLUTION CLASSIC 4 PLUS availability, colors, and accessory options.
