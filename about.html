About Pocono Golf Carts

Your trusted partner in eco-friendly transportation solutions since our founding. We're committed to providing premium electric vehicles that combine performance, reliability, and environmental responsibility.

Our Story

Pocono Golf Carts has been at the forefront of the electric vehicle revolution, specializing in neighborhood electric vehicles (NEVs) that provide sustainable transportation solutions for communities across the region. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make eco-friendly transportation accessible, reliable, and enjoyable for everyone.

We pride ourselves on offering a comprehensive selection of premium electric vehicles from leading manufacturers like DENAGO and EVOLUTION. Each vehicle in our lineup is carefully selected for its quality, performance, and innovative features that meet the diverse needs of our customers.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional electric vehicles and outstanding customer service while promoting sustainable transportation solutions that benefit both our customers and the environment.

Our Values

  • Quality: We offer only the highest quality electric vehicles from trusted manufacturers.
  • Service: Our commitment to customer satisfaction extends beyond the sale with comprehensive support and maintenance services.
  • Innovation: We stay at the cutting edge of electric vehicle technology to bring you the latest advancements.
  • Sustainability: We're dedicated to promoting eco-friendly transportation that reduces environmental impact.
